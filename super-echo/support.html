How to Play

Super Echo is a memory sequence game. A random sequence of cells lights up on a 4×4 grid — your goal is to watch carefully and tap each cell back in the exact same order.

Watch the Sequence

At the start of each round, cells on the 4×4 grid will highlight green one by one. Pay close attention to which cells light up and the order they appear in.

Tap to Replicate

Once all cells have finished highlighting, it's your turn. Tap the cells in the exact same order they were shown. A correct tap lights the cell green — a wrong tap ends the game immediately.

Level Up

Every 6 successful rounds, the sequence grows by one cell. It starts at 4 cells and can grow all the way up to 12. Each level is harder than the last — stay focused.

Game Over

One wrong tap ends the game. Your final score, streak, and sequence length are displayed on the game over screen.

Start a New Game

When the game ends, tap PLAY AGAIN on the game over screen to start a fresh game.

FAQ

Is Super Echo free to play?
Super Echo is a paid app available on the App Store. There are no ads, in-app purchases, or subscriptions — it is a one-time purchase.
How long does a sequence get?
Sequences start at 4 cells and increase by one every 6 successful rounds, up to a maximum of 12 cells. The game ends when you make your first wrong tap.
Are the sequences random every game?
Yes. Every sequence is randomly generated, so no two games are the same. The cells that light up and their order are always different.
What counts as a correct tap?
You must tap the cells in the exact order they were highlighted during the sequence. The position matters — tapping the right cell at the wrong point in the sequence counts as a mistake.
Is there a time limit for tapping?
No. Once the sequence finishes playing, you can take as long as you need to tap your answer. There is no countdown or time pressure.
What does the streak counter track?
The streak counter tracks how many rounds you've completed successfully in a row during the current game. Every 6 streak points, the sequence length increases by one.
Can I restart mid-game?
Yes — tap PLAY AGAIN on the game over screen to start a fresh game. You can also restart at any time by closing and reopening the app.
The app isn't behaving as expected. What should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app first. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. If it still occurs, please contact us at ian.jyz@gmail.com with a description of what happened and your device model.
